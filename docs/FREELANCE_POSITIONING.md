# Freelance Positioning

## Core positioning

AI-powered systems builder for creators, local businesses, and modern service brands.

## What that means in practice

- Build or tighten the website front door
- Improve lead capture and follow-up
- Set up practical automation
- Integrate AI where it saves time or increases responsiveness
- Build fast MVPs and prototypes

## Tone

- Direct
- Useful
- Modern
- Credible
- Non-corporate

## Copy principles

- Avoid fake scale, fake case studies, and inflated promises
- Lead with practical outcomes, not AI hype
- Use service language normal businesses understand
- Keep discovery CTAs invitational instead of aggressive

## Homepage headline options

1. Launch pages, lead systems, and AI workflows that help small teams move faster.
2. Practical AI systems for creators, local businesses, and modern service brands.
3. Better websites, better follow-up, and smarter workflows without the agency bloat.

## Trust-building copy themes

- Clear scope before expansion
- Honest pricing ranges
- Fast iteration
- Real working deliverables
- AI used as leverage, not theater

## Audience-specific angles

### Creators

Turn audience attention into cleaner offers, landing pages, and lightweight systems.

### Local businesses

Capture more inquiries and reduce manual admin without replacing the whole business.

### Agencies

Use Killough Works as overflow execution for landing pages, prototypes, or automation builds.

### Startups

Get a believable prototype or acquisition surface live before overspending.
