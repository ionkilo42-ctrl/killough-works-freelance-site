## 2. Scope of this statement

This statement applies to the Killough Works marketing website at https://killough.works, including primary pages such as the homepage, payment hub, demos catalog, and intake flow.

- Third-party checkout pages operated by Stripe

---

## 4. Measures we take

Killough Works works toward accessibility by:

- Using semantic HTML where practicable
- Providing text alternatives for meaningful images
- Maintaining readable color contrast in core layouts
- Supporting keyboard navigation for primary interactions
- Labeling form fields in the intake flow
- Testing layouts across common viewport sizes
- Reviewing new pages before release

Because the Site evolves, new issues may appear. We treat accessibility as a maintenance priority, not a one-time checkbox.

## 5. Known limitations

As of the effective date above, known or potential limitations may include:

| Area | Limitation |
|------|------------|
| Demo catalog | Interactive demos prioritize illustration of business concepts and may not meet full production accessibility standards |
| Third-party embeds | Payment, booking, and external APIs are not fully controlled by Killough Works |
| Decorative mockups | Some gallery images are illustrative before/after mockups with limited alt-text detail |
| Live components | Rapid iteration may temporarily introduce focus-order or contrast issues before correction |
| Video deliverables | Client-facing Loom-style reviews may require captions upon request |

We are working to reduce these limitations over time.

## 6. Compatibility

The Site is designed to work with current versions of major browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge, and with common assistive technologies where practicable.

Older browsers or uncommon assistive technology combinations may experience reduced support.
