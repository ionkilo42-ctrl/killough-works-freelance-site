# Freelance Launch Plan

## Positioning Statement

Killough Works is an AI-powered systems builder for creators, local businesses, and modern service brands that need sharper positioning, better lead capture, useful automation, and fast prototype execution.

## Offer Ladder

1. Strategy Call
   - 30-minute discovery call
   - Goal: qualify fast and identify the bottleneck
2. Launch Page Sprint
   - Fast homepage or landing page with clear CTA and trust copy
3. Lead Capture / Follow-Up Build
   - Intake, routing, auto-response, and lightweight sales workflow
4. Automation / AI Workflow Setup
   - Practical workflow improvements for repetitive internal tasks
5. MVP / Prototype Build
   - Working vertical slice for a product or service concept
6. Ongoing Optimization Retainer
   - Monthly improvement, iteration, maintenance, and experiments

## Pricing Suggestions

- Strategy call: free or $75-$150 if demand starts getting noisy
- Launch page sprint: $750-$1,500
- Lead capture build: $1,500-$3,500
- Automation setup: $1,500-$4,000
- MVP / prototype: $3,500-$8,500+
- Monthly optimization retainer: $500-$2,000 depending on scope

## First-Client Acquisition Strategy

1. Tighten the site so it clearly explains what you do and who it is for.
2. Add one working booking link and one intake flow.
3. Reach out to warm contacts first: creators, local businesses, restaurants, agencies, and founders already in motion.
4. Use short audits as wedges, not giant free strategy decks.
5. Close the first 2-3 projects quickly, then replace placeholders with real case studies.
