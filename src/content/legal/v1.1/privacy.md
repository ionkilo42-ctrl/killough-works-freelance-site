## 3. Information we collect

### 3.1 Information you provide directly

When you submit the Site intake form or otherwise contact us, we may collect:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Identity & contact | Name, email address |
| Business information | Business name, website or social link |
| Project information | Service tier interest, description of problems or goals |
| Communications | Emails, messages, files, screenshots, and notes you send |
| Payment support info | Billing name, email used at checkout, transaction references you provide |

The intake form currently collects: name, email, business name, optional website/social link, selected offer interest, and a free-text project summary.

### 3.2 Information collected automatically

When you visit the Site, certain technical information may be processed by our hosting and infrastructure providers, such as:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device type
- Pages visited and approximate visit time
- Referring URL
- Basic server logs and error logs

**Analytics:** We do **not** currently use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or similar marketing analytics on the Site. If we add analytics later, we will update this policy and, where required, provide a cookie notice.

### 3.3 Payment information

Payments are processed by **Stripe**. Killough Works does not receive or store your full payment card number. Stripe may provide us with limited payment metadata such as payment status, amount, currency, transaction ID, payer email, and timestamp.

### 3.4 Client project materials

If you become a client, we may receive additional information needed to perform services, such as platform access details, brand assets, customer form submissions from systems we build, or workflow data. We treat that information as confidential project data and use it only to deliver agreed services unless you direct otherwise.

## 7. Data retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above:

| Data type | Retention guideline |
|-----------|---------------------|
